Why Remove Horizontal Markings?

Removing old horizontal markings is often necessary to renew existing markings or change the traffic organization in industrial facilities. Proper removal ensures a well-prepared surface for new applications, improving durability and appearance.

Removing Lines with Pressurized Water

Using water under high pressure, up to 2500 bar, this method effectively removes markings from asphalt or concrete surfaces. Environmentally friendly and non-abrasive, it is ideal for hard, rubbed surfaces and concrete blocks.

Chemical Line Removal

Chemical removal is performed on concrete and epoxy floors using specialized agents that loosen and dissolve paint. This method does not damage the floor and is ideal for rented facilities with strict return conditions.

Methods for Removing Road Markings

  • Mechanical: Using diamond grinders, this method removes markings but alters the substrate structure.
  • Chemical: Involves applying dissolving agents without damaging the substrate.
  • Pressurized Water: Removes thin and thick-layer markings without chemicals, making it eco-friendly and substrate-safe.
